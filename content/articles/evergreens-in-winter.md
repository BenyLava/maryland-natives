## Why winter is a good time to learn

With most deciduous trees bare, the evergreens stand out:

- Their silhouettes are easier to see from a distance.  
- Needles, cones, and buds are more visible without a wall of leaves in the way.  
- Snow or frost can highlight the structure of branches and twigs.

## Three details to notice

1. **Needle arrangement** – are needles in clusters, pairs, or singles?  
2. **Cone shape and size** – small and round, long and pendant, or tiny and tucked against the twig?  
3. **Overall form** – tall and spire‑like, or broad and layered?
